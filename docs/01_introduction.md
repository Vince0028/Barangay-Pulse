# 1. Project Introduction

## What is BrgyPulse?

BrgyPulse is a decentralized civic reporting and emergency management application tailored for local Philippine communities (Barangays). The primary goal is to empower citizens to easily report local issues and to ensure that during natural disasters, critical communication lines remain open even when standard cellular and Wi-Fi networks fail.

BrgyPulse is split into two primary operational modes:
1. **Everyday Civic Mode:** Designed for routine reports such as uncollected trash, illegal parking, noise complaints, and infrastructure damage.
2. **Emergency/Disaster Mode:** A vital mode during typhoons and calamities. It features live SOS reporting, flood mapping, and an offline mesh network protocol.
